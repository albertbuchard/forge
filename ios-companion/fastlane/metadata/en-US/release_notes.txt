Forge Companion 1.0.11 adds persistent on-device diagnostics for TestFlight and
release builds, plus a copyable Diagnostics > Logs surface so location-label
failures can be pasted directly from the app. The movement timeline now logs
the full stay labeling and location creation path, including draft opening,
create taps, pairing resolution, remote place creation, local place storage,
stay relabeling, and any failure message along the way.
