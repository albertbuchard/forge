Forge Companion 1.0.34 fixes movement timeline loading against Forge servers that
still enforce the 120-item mobile page limit. The app now pages deeper history in
compatible chunks instead of sending one oversized request, so early April boxes
can load without a status 400 error.

Normal WebView loading state messages are no longer mislabeled as errors when the
loading error is nil.

The movement timeline now keeps row rendering virtualized on iPhone and web while
preserving the existing background hour alignment.
