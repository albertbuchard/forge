Forge Companion 1.0.10 fixes the iOS location creation flow from unlabeled
stays: the second modal now saves correctly, closes after creation, reapplies
the new label to the stay, and stops preloading irrelevant repair/system tags
into the location form. This keeps the location draft focused on the place
name, coordinates, and optional category tags. TestFlight releases now also
skip the App Store media capture step instead of blocking on screenshot work.
