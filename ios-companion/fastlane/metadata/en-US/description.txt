Forge Companion is the native iPhone bridge for Forge. It pairs with your Forge runtime,
requests Apple permissions directly, imports HealthKit sleep and workouts, passively
captures movement history, and keeps your reflected life data synced back into Forge.

The companion is designed to stay low-friction. After pairing, it should handle sync,
movement capture, and HealthKit import quietly in the background while still letting you
review movement timelines, sync health data manually when needed, and open the full Forge
experience from the phone.
